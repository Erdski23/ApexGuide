Winning Tips

  • Stay with your squad. Its crucial to be playing with your team and not run off to go get kills or loot, to win fights you need to have your whole team there.
  • Rotate early into safe zones is great, but sometimes you'll have bad positioning based off of where you decided to land. Thats when is useful to have a diverse team and have a movement legend for better rotates
  • Use high ground whenever possible. Height will help in your fights with having better angles of fire or more use for abilites like throwing caustics gas traps down onto enemies hugging a cliff.
  • Communicate enemy locations clearly. If you are playing solo there are all sorts of useful pings on the ping wheel tp provide clear call outs. If you have mics to communicate with open comms its good to call out when you are rotating, enemies postion, if they are damaged, and if you are healing
