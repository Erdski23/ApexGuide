Current Meta Weapons

  • R-301 Carbine: it is the best mid range gun in the game with highest damage output, low recoil, and high ammo capacity
  • Volt SMG: it is a consistent smg with very good firerate and damage and is easy enough to control with a good base sight
  • R-99: if you are more skilled the R-99 is for you, it is a better volt for firerate but is significantly harder to control with the most recoil in the game
  • Peacekeeper: Peacekeeper is a soild shotgun and can have very high damage output but can be inconsistent with pellets hitting and charging your shots for a concentration of those pellets
  • Eva-8: it is an easier shotgun to use then the peacekeeper and can also be better with its lower damage for a shotgun but much higher firerate and more consistent damage
  • Wingman: the wignman is a high damaging revolver that is hard to hit shots with but does 55 base damage to the body and does high headshot damage, so hard to use but very big pay off if you can hit shots
